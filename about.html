About Us

The story of how and why we began

Driftwood Atlas was born from a love of texture, tide, and time — from the way the ocean carves beauty into imperfection. We find inspiration in the soft grain of salt-smoothed wood, in the curve of something once wild, now at peace. Each piece we craft carries that same quiet rhythm — shaped, not forced, by hand and by heart.

Our work celebrates the freedom of the sea and the calm it leaves behind. The scent of brine and pine, the touch of weathered wood, the hush of the horizon — these are the stories we tell. Driftwood Atlas is not just about what we make, but what nature remembers.

The reason we create handbound leather sketbooks (or journals) is because we started from sketching beside driftwood sitting on the shore. Inspired by the driftwood, the ideas flowed in to our founder's sketchbook.

We like the idea of including our 'roots' like that!
